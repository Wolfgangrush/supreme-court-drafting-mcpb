# format-from-user.md — SLP (Civil) · canonical drafting baseline

**Status:** Pre-populated with the validated Bombay HC Nagpur drafting style adapted for Supreme Court conventions. Adapt for your AOR practice if needed. **Drafter will use this baseline if you do not customize.**

Style is not copyrighted; only verbatim drafted prose is. The baseline below reflects standard Indian legal-drafting conventions at the Supreme Court of India.

---

## Section 1 — Synopsis opener (canonical baseline)

```
It is most respectfully submitted that the present Special Leave Petition arises out of the impugned final judgment and order dated <DD.MM.YYYY> passed by the Hon'ble High Court of [State] at [Place] in [Case Type & Number], whereby the Hon'ble High Court [briefly state the impugned outcome — dismissing / allowing / setting aside / etc.].
```

## Section 2 — List of Dates header

```
LIST OF DATES AND EVENTS

| Date | Event |
| ---- | ----- |
| <DD.MM.YYYY> | [first event] |
| <DD.MM.YYYY> | [intervening event] |
| <DD.MM.YYYY> | [date of impugned judgment / order] |
```

## Section 3 — Cause Title format

```
IN THE SUPREME COURT OF INDIA
CIVIL APPELLATE JURISDICTION

Special Leave Petition (Civil) No. _____ of [Year]

(Arising out of impugned final judgment and order dated <DD.MM.YYYY> passed by
the Hon'ble High Court of [State] at [Place] in [Case Type & Number])

  IN THE MATTER OF:

  [Petitioner Name]                                ... Petitioner
                                                   (Original [status — Plaintiff / Defendant /
                                                              Appellant / Respondent etc.])

                          VERSUS

  [Respondent Name]                                ... Respondent
                                                   (Original [status])
```

## Section 4 — Statement of Facts opening

```
The Petitioner most respectfully submits as under:

1. [Opening factual paragraph identifying the parties' status in the lower court and the substantive cause of action.]

2. [Chronological narrative paragraphs. Each fact paragraph numbered. Every documentary fact ends with the inline annexure marker: "...a true copy whereof is annexed hereto and marked as ANNEXURE P-[N]."]
```

## Section 5 — Grounds opener and numbering

```
GROUNDS

A. BECAUSE the impugned judgment is contrary to the settled position of law that [legal proposition].

B. BECAUSE the Hon'ble High Court has failed to consider [factual / legal element].

C. BECAUSE the impugned judgment occasions grave miscarriage of justice in that [specific harm].

[Each ground a distinct paragraph; lettered A, B, C ... in SC convention.]
```

## Section 6 — Main Prayer opener

```
In the premises aforesaid, it is most respectfully prayed that this Hon'ble Court may graciously be pleased to:

(a) Grant special leave to appeal under Article 136 of the Constitution of India against the impugned judgment and order dated <DD.MM.YYYY> passed by the Hon'ble High Court of [State] at [Place] in [Case Type & Number];

(b) [Substantive relief on grant of leave — e.g., "Allow the appeal and set aside the impugned judgment / order"];

(c) Pass any other or further order(s) as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper in the facts and circumstances of the case and in the interest of justice.
```

## Section 7 — Catchall (final prayer clause)

```
Pass any other or further order(s) as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper in the facts and circumstances of the case and in the interest of justice.
```

## Section 8 — AOR Certificate block (verbatim per Order IV Rule 1(c) SC Rules 2013)

```
CERTIFICATE BY ADVOCATE-ON-RECORD

I, [AOR Name], Advocate-on-Record for the Petitioner, do hereby certify that this Special Leave Petition is confined to the pleadings before the Hon'ble High Court / Court / Tribunal whose order is challenged and the other statutory requirements of the Supreme Court Rules, 2013 have been complied with.

NEW DELHI                                  [AOR NAME], Advocate-on-Record
DATE: <DD.MM.YYYY>                         Registration No. [AOR Code]
                                           COUNSEL FOR THE PETITIONER
```

## Section 9 — Affidavit verification (verbatim per CPC Order 19)

```
AFFIDAVIT

I, [Petitioner Name], aged about [age] years, Occu. [occupation], R/o [address], the Petitioner above-named, do hereby solemnly affirm and state on oath as under:

1. That I am the Petitioner in the above-titled matter and am well-conversant with the facts of the case.

2. That the contents of the accompanying Special Leave Petition from paragraphs 1 to [N] are true to my personal knowledge / belief; the contents of paragraphs [N+1] to [M] are based on information received from [source] and believed by me to be true.

3. That no part of this affidavit is false and nothing material has been concealed therefrom.

Verified at NEW DELHI this [Day] day of [Month, Year].

                                           ____________________
                                                  (DEPONENT)
```
