## Section 1 — Synopsis opener (Criminal SLP)

```
It is most respectfully submitted that the present Special Leave Petition (Criminal) arises out of the impugned final judgment and order dated <DD.MM.YYYY> passed by the Hon'ble High Court of [State] at [Place] in [Criminal Appeal / Criminal Writ Petition / etc.] No. [____] of [Year], whereby the Hon'ble High Court [briefly state the impugned outcome — confirming the conviction / setting aside acquittal / dismissing the appeal / etc.]. The Petitioner is presently [in jail / on bail / on anticipatory bail / not in custody], having been granted bail by the [court] on <DD.MM.YYYY> in [Case Type & Number].
```

## Section 3 — Custody-status opening paragraph (Criminal SLP-specific)

```
1. At the threshold, it is respectfully submitted that the Petitioner is presently [in jail at [Jail Name], serving the impugned sentence / on bail granted by the [court] on <DD.MM.YYYY> in [Case Type & Number] / on anticipatory bail granted by the [court] on <DD.MM.YYYY>]. [Where in custody: "The Petitioner has been in custody since <DD.MM.YYYY>, a period of approximately [N] months / years."]
```

## Section 4 — Grounds (Criminal SLP-specific)

```
GROUNDS

A. BECAUSE the impugned judgment of conviction is contrary to the evidence on record in that [specific evidentiary defect].

B. BECAUSE the Hon'ble High Court has failed to appreciate that [defence evidence / mitigating factor / procedural irregularity].

C. BECAUSE the conviction is liable to be set aside on account of [legal defect — e.g., violation of fair trial / non-compliance with procedural safeguards / wrongful framing of charges].

D. BECAUSE the sentence imposed is disproportionate to the offence proved and is liable to be modified [where sentencing is challenged].

[For appeals against acquittal — additional grounds addressing the appellate court's reluctance to disturb a considered acquittal absent perversity / illegality.]

[Dual-citation throughout: every reference to Cr.P.C. paired with the corresponding BNSS section; every reference to IPC paired with BNS; every reference to IEA paired with BSA.]
```

## Section 5 — Interim Prayer (Criminal SLP-specific)

```
(b) Pending hearing and final disposal of the present Special Leave Petition, this Hon'ble Court may graciously be pleased to:

    (i) Suspend the operation of the impugned sentence dated <DD.MM.YYYY> passed by the Hon'ble High Court of [State] at [Place] in [Case Type & Number]; AND

    (ii) Direct the release of the Petitioner on bail on such terms and conditions as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit to impose;
```
