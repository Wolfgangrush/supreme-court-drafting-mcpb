## Section 1 — Statutory Opening (Civil / Criminal Transfer)

```
For Civil Transfer:
TRANSFER PETITION (CIVIL) UNDER SECTION 25 OF THE CODE OF CIVIL PROCEDURE, 1908 FOR TRANSFER OF [Case Type & Number] PENDING BEFORE THE [Court Name] AT [Place], [State] TO THE [Receiving Court Name] AT [Receiving Place], [Receiving State].

For Criminal Transfer:
TRANSFER PETITION (CRIMINAL) UNDER SECTION 406 OF THE BHARATIYA NAGARIK SURAKSHA SANHITA, 2023 (corresponding to Section 406 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973) FOR TRANSFER OF [Case Type & Number] PENDING BEFORE THE [Court Name] AT [Place], [State] TO THE [Receiving Court Name] AT [Receiving Place], [Receiving State].
```

## Section 2 — Grounds for Transfer (canonical phrasing)

```
GROUNDS FOR TRANSFER

(a) The Petitioner is unable to attend the existing court at [Place] on account of [specific factual basis — medical condition / personal hardship / distance / etc.].

(b) [Where fair-trial apprehension is invoked:] There is a reasonable apprehension that the Petitioner will not receive a fair trial at the existing court, in that [specific facts grounding the apprehension].

(c) The convenience of witnesses and parties requires transfer of the proceeding to [Receiving Court Name] at [Receiving Place].

(d) [Where concurrent / connected proceedings are invoked:] Connected proceedings being [Case Type & Number] are pending before the Hon'ble [Receiving Court Name], and consolidation in the interest of justice requires the transfer sought herein.
```

## Section 3 — Main Prayer

```
In the premises aforesaid, it is most respectfully prayed that this Hon'ble Court may graciously be pleased to:

(a) Transfer [Case Type & Number] pending before the [Court Name] at [Place], [State] to the [Receiving Court Name] at [Receiving Place], [Receiving State];

(b) Pending hearing and final disposal of the present Transfer Petition, stay all further proceedings in [Case Type & Number] before the [Court Name] at [Place];

(c) Pass any other or further order(s) as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper.
```
